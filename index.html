Corporate Overview

Xorvila Hub was founded with a singular directive: To engineer the most resilient infrastructure on the internet.

A Decade of Infrastructure Excellence

Established in 2015, Xorvila Hub began as an internal solution for high-frequency trading firms requiring microsecond latency and absolute zero downtime. We recognized that the broader enterprise market was burdened by opaque pricing, legacy hardware, and unacceptable support tiers from legacy providers.

Today, Xorvila Hub operates a proprietary global backbone connecting 12 Tier-4 equivalent data centers. We serve over 50,000 active deployments, ranging from massive multi-national SaaS platforms to critical governmental data repositories. Our continuous reinvestment in NVMe, DDR5, and AMD EPYC architectures ensures our clients always possess a competitive technological advantage.
